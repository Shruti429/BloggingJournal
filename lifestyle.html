30 Best Lifestyle Blogs To Follow in 2021

Do you follow the latest fashion, food, and travel trends? Do you enjoy sharing your experiences with other people? If that’s the case, then you can become a lifestyle blogger. Naturally, to get started, you’ll need to learn more about running and operating a blog.

That’s why I decided to create a list of the best lifestyle blogs. While it wasn’t an easy task, I managed to select platforms that I believe can inspire and give you useful advice about how to lay a foundation and start working on your own unique version of a lifestyle blog.

On top of that, these websites can help you to improve your quality of life and teach you how to make the most out of every day. So, let’s dive in!

30 inspiring examples of lifestyle blogs

I had a hard time deciding which platforms to include in this article simply because the web hosts countless high-quality lifestyle blogs, each one unique in its own way. This list could have been endless, but I chose 30 blogs that truly piqued my interest.

So, if you need some life inspiration, help, or tips and tricks, the following lifestyle blogs can be a great help:

1. Camille Styles

Based in Austin, Texas, Camille Styles offers us a fresh and new approach to lifestyle, fashion, and home decoration. Along with a team, she runs her lifestyle blog where she shares her take and ideas about wellness, food, design, style, and travel. Camille is also the author of the bestselling book “Camille Styles Entertaining.”

2. Quintessence

Launched in 2010 by Stacey Bewkes, Quintessence is a lifestyle blog that guides us through arts, architecture, design, travel, fashion, and interior design while offering original content for anyone looking for inspiration.

3. The Skinny Confidential

Lauryn Evarts launched this lifestyle blog with the goal of promoting healthy living. If you’re interested in her unique take on fitness and wellness, you should definitely read some of her posts. Additionally, Lauryn has a podcast where she introduces expert guests to share their advice.

4. The Blonde Abroad

If you have the travel bug, then Kiki’s lifestyle blog is an optimal place to explore potential destinations, read about her experiences, and more. Also, she shares breathtaking pictures of all the places she visits, allowing us to live vicariously through her travels.

5. Wit & Delight

Wit & Delight is an online hub filled with blog posts about lifestyle, fashion, food & drinks, and parenthood. Run by Kate Arends, this lifestyle blog shares her personal stories, struggles, and experiences. Through her immersive posts, we can learn so much about ourselves and improve our lifestyle.

6. Julie Blanner

Online since 2009, Julie Blanner shares her favorite recipes, decor, and DIY ideas. So, if you’re looking to try new food and drink recipes or you want to redecorate your home, this blog will certainly inspire you.

7. Jess Ann Kirby

Jess Ann Kirby used to work in HR, but she left her 9-5 job and started a new life, prompting the development of her lifestyle blog. Today, through her posts, she encourages people and provokes deep conversations we all need to have with ourselves from time to time.

8. Lifestyle and Berries

Lifestyle and Berries is a blog focusing on health, lifestyle, weight-loss, and decoration. As you scroll through the platform, you’ll also notice that it covers other themes, like fashion trends. This blog produces daily posts filled with useful advice, delicious recipes, and healthy lifestyle tips.

9. The Pioneer Woman

Ree Drummond is a blogger, writer, photographer, and mother. She turned her passions into a business, and today, she runs one of the biggest lifestyle blogs. The platform provides its readers with an abundance of high-quality content regarding food, beauty, home & style, news, and entertainment.

10. Amber Fillerup

Formerly known as the Barefoot Blonde, this lifestyle blog follows the life of Amber Fillerup Clark, an Arizona-based blogger. In addition to sharing personal stories, Amber also gives beauty, travel, and fashion tips & tricks.
